Eternal Night

Video-game - 2D Platformer

Description

My first video-game platformer 2D project. The player controls Nyx, a bat that sets off on a quest to find the sun that has disappeared from the forest. This game, initially created as a short animated film, aims to invent new movement palettes.

For example, Nyx can glide over a cliff, climb vertical walls of the level and grab onto horizontal branches suspended in the void. (㇏(•̀ ᢍ •́ )ノ)
