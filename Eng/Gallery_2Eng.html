Neon flowers

2D Animation

Description

Neon flowers is a project of video mapping started at the end of 2024. Neon and luminous flowers dance and change their colors according to the music, creating choreographies.

The idea is to create a video projected on the walls of a room or on the exterior walls of a building (somewhat like at Disneyland castle during a evening show). This ambitious project is currently in development. ✿
